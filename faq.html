Frequently Asked Questions

Most asked questions, answered

What is a Digital Business Ally?

A Digital Business Ally is a remote professional who provides administrative, technical, or creative support to individuals or businesses.

What are the benefits of a Digital Business Ally?

A Digital Business Ally saves you time, reduces workload, and provides specialized skills without the need for a full-time employee.

How do I ensure my data is secure with my Digital Business Ally?

Use secure communication tools, sign confidentiality agreements, and provide access only to necessary information.

How do I onboard a Digital Ally effectively?

Provide clear instructions, access to necessary tools, and a detailed overview of your expectations and processes.

What tools are recommended to help collaborate with my Digital Business Ally?

Tools like Google Workspace, Slack, Trello, or Asana can help streamline communication and task management

Can a Digital Business Ally work in my time zone?

Depending on what time zone, it is possible to be flexible

How do I ensure my Digital Business Ally understands my brand?

Share brand guidelines, tone of voice documents, and examples of past work to help align with your vision.
